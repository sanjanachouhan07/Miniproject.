About Ludhiana

Ludhiana, known as the Manchester of India, is the largest city in Punjab and an important industrial center. With a rich cultural heritage and modern amenities, it offers visitors a unique blend of tradition and progress.

From historical sites and religious places to shopping hubs and food streets, Ludhiana has something for everyone.
